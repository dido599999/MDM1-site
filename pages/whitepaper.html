1. Project Overview

MDm1 is a decentralized digital ecosystem built on the Polygon network. The project aims to combine education, community participation, transparency, and real-world social impact through a long-term evolving platform.

The current phase focuses on building a transparent foundation, educational resources, and a sustainable community-driven environment without unrealistic promises or centralized dependence.

2. Vision

The long-term vision of MDm1 is to create an open ecosystem where users can learn, participate, contribute, and benefit from educational, community, and future real-world initiatives connected to the project.

Some ecosystem features described in this document represent future goals and community-oriented directions currently under development.
